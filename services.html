Other Services

  • Low-Cost Vaccine Clinics

    • FVHA provides low-cost vaccine clinics to ensure pets are healthy and up-to-date on vaccinations. These clinics are open to the public and require an appointment. For pricing and scheduling information, please Click Here.

  • Lost and Found Pet Services

    • FVHA offers lost and found pet services to help reunite lost pets with their families. If you have lost or found a pet, please visit our Stray Pets Facebook Page to see if we have your furry friend.

  • Spay and Neuter Assistance Program

    • FVHA offers spay and neuter assistance programs for those in need of financial assistance. And is available for residents of Outagamie, Calumet, and Winnebago counties. For more information and to see pricing, and make an appointment, please fill out the form Here.
