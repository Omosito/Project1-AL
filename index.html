Fox Valley Humane Association

Who is FVHA?

The Fox Valley Humane Association is a animal shelter in Appleton, Wisconsin.

What do they do?

Their mission is to remove barriers for pets and their people by providing continusue care, education, and connections to community resources.

These animals include:

  • Dogs
  • Cats
  • Rabbits
  • Guinea pigs
  • Birds
  • Ferrets
