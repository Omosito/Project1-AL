Donate

FVHA is a non-profit organization that operates on your donations. There are options for one time or monthly donations that can help with treating an animal's disease, sponsoring an adoption, or simply feeding the animals.

Volunteer

Volunteering is one of many ways you can help the Fox Valley Humane Association. There are many options, including:

  • Front Desk Assistant
  • Dog Walking
  • Cat Socialization
  • Shelter Maintenance

And many more!

Volunteer Page

Become a Foster

Fostering is a way for the FVHA to expand the number of animals it can care for by placing them in the care and shelter of others. Foster parents for these animals must simply welcome these animals into their homes and care for them until a permanent home can be found, all while the FVHA provides food, supplies, and other support to help a foster parent care for them.
